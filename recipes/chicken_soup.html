How to make Chicken Soup

Description

Filled with onions, carrots, and celery, feel free to add all your favorite vegetables including leeks, cucumber, parsnips, potatoes, and kale.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make your broth
  2. Cook the vegetables
  3. Strain the broth
  4. Shred the chicken
  5. Simmer & serve
