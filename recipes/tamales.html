How to make Tamales

Description

Traditional Mexican dish made with a corn based dough mixture that is filled with various meats or beans and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Masa Harina
  • 3 cups broth (beef, chicken, or vegetable broth)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Soak the corn husks
  2. Prepare desired filling
  3. Make the masa dough
  4. Assemble the tamales
  5. Tie the tamales
  6. Cook on the stove-top or Instant Pot
